About Me

I’m a data science graduate who loves translating complex systems into meaningful human stories. My work explores the intersection of data, design, and dialogue — from interactive analysis to public communication that makes ideas come alive. I have a unique background in visual art, writing, marketing, full stack development, artificial intelligence and data visualization which allows me to bring my ideas to life from concept through to production. I've worked in multiple industries including finance, healthcare data, utilities, construction and consulting. I view data as a beautiful, fascinating creative opportunity to explore the world in search of deeper understanding.
